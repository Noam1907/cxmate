## The Problem: A $4.7T Market With No Middle Ground

89% of consumers switch to a competitor after a poor experience. Companies know customer experience determines revenue — but the tools to do anything about it are built for enterprises with dedicated CX teams, six-figure budgets, and 12-24 week implementations.

**The result:** Every B2B company without a CX expert — seed-stage to Series B, 2 to 500 people — is running their customer journey on gut feel, spreadsheets, and founder memory. They don't have "bad CX." They have *accidental CX* — a journey that happened to them instead of one they designed.

There is nothing between "wing it" and "Gainsight at $50K/year." That gap is where CX Mate lives.

---

## What CX Mate Is

**An AI-powered CX intelligence system — like having a CCXP-certified expert on your team, available in minutes instead of months.**

One conversation. Your company name, website, and honest answers about where you are. CX Mate auto-enriches your profile from your website, walks you through maturity-adaptive onboarding, and generates:

- **A complete journey map** — every meaningful moment from first touch to advocacy, with risk levels and evidence annotations tied to *your* specific situation
- **A CX Intelligence Report** — hard truths about your gaps, with transparent revenue impact projections (formulas visible, assumptions disclosed)
- **A prioritized playbook** — what to do Monday, with AI tool recommendations, email templates, and effort estimates

No CRM integration required. No setup. No CX expertise needed.

## The CX Intelligence Stack™ — 7-Layer Architecture

What makes CX Mate fundamentally different from asking ChatGPT or hiring a consultant:

```
 L6  Outcome Intelligence     What actually worked — the learning loop          [FUTURE MOAT]
 L5  External Signals         Regulatory, social, funding-round timing signals
 L4  User Intelligence        Behavior data, CRM integration, aggregate patterns
 L3  Technology Intelligence  What tools + AI can do RIGHT NOW                  [FRESHNESS]
 L2  Market Intelligence      Competitor gaps, benchmarks, hiring signals
 L1  Methodology Intelligence CCXP frameworks, 15 expert models, research       [DEPTH]
 L0  Input Foundation         33 structured fields + live website enrichment     [THE MOAT]
```

**Why this matters:** A raw LLM gets a company name and returns plausible-looking generic advice. CX Mate cross-references 33 structured intelligence fields against methodology from 15 named CX expert frameworks, real competitive data, and maturity-specific analysis modes. The quality gap is in the *inputs and the structure*, not the model.

**The three-tier moat:**
1. **Data moat** (future) — Aggregate outcome patterns across users. Impossible to replicate without the same user base tracked over time.
2. **Workflow moat** (strong today) — Sales promise → CS handoff → journey → outcome tracking. Leave CX Mate, lose the thread.
3. **Intelligence architecture** (defensible) — The 7-layer stack. Replicable in 6-12 months by a well-funded competitor, but no one is building it.

### Why the gap exists now

1. **AI made it possible.** Structured CX intelligence that required a $150K consultant + 3 months can now be generated in minutes — *if* you build the right knowledge architecture underneath. The LLM is the engine; the 7-layer stack is the fuel.

2. **The "CX-before-you-need-CX" moment.** Companies that build CX foundations early (pre-launch to first customers) avoid the reactive firefighting that costs 5-10x more to fix later. Aberdeen Group data: journey management drives 200% employee engagement, 3.5x referrals, 18x faster sales cycles.

3. **SMBs lose 1-9% of total revenue to operational fragmentation.** That's not a CX problem they named — it's a CX problem they're living. CX Mate names it for them.

4. **Every competitor is reactive.** ChurnZero, Totango, and Planhat manage existing customer health. No one helps you *design the journey before it goes wrong.* CX Mate is proactive by architecture, not by feature.

### The "Why not just ask ChatGPT?" answer

A founder who knows enough to write a perfect CX prompt to Gemini probably already has a CX director. The entire ICP is everyone who doesn't. CX Mate's value is that it *knows which questions to ask*, asks them in the right sequence, and turns the answers into a structured intelligence system — not a one-time text response.
