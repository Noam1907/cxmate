## Writing Rules for This Email

- Never open with "Thank you for your time" — it's what every SaaS vendor writes
- The personalization hook must reference something they actually said, not something generic
- One link, one CTA — do not give them three options
- Keep the total email under 250 words (not counting the P.S.)
- The P.S. should feel like a thought you had after hitting send, not a marketing add-on
- No bullet lists in the opening or the CTA — save bullets for the middle summary only
- Tone: like a founder emailing a founder, not a salesperson following up a lead

---

## Timing Notes

- Send within 2 hours. The demo is freshest in their mind right now.
- If it's after 6pm their local time, schedule for 8am their time the next morning — not 11pm
- If they didn't respond within 48 hours, one follow-up only: "Did the link work?" — short, specific, easy to reply to
- After 5 days of no response: pause. Re-engage only if a new trigger event appears (funding announcement, job post for CS, churn spike)
