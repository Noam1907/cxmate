## The Problem

Your customers have a journey whether you designed it or not.

Most founders know which customers churned. Almost none know which moment caused it. You're running on gut feel, Slack DMs, and whatever your CRM can tell you — which isn't enough.

The CX platforms designed for this (Gainsight, Qualtrics, Medallia) cost six figures and take six months to implement. Built for enterprises with dedicated CX teams. Not for you.

So you stay stuck between "we should probably fix this" and "we don't have time."

## What CX Mate Does

**In one conversation, CX Mate builds you:**

1. **A journey map** — Every meaningful moment across your full customer lifecycle, from first touch to renewal. Mapped specifically to your stage, your customers, your challenges. Not a template.

2. **A CX Intelligence Report** — Where you're losing customers and why. Revenue at risk, quantified. Impact projections with transparent math (your data + industry benchmarks, both labeled).

3. **A playbook** — Prioritized actions for every high-risk moment. Effort estimates. AI tool recommendations per action. Evidence connecting every insight back to your real situation.

**Time to generate: under 10 minutes of onboarding.**
