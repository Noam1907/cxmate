## What Makes CX Mate Different (Not Just Cheaper)

1. **Intelligence, not data collection.** Qualtrics collects feedback. CX Mate generates a diagnosis — what's at risk, where deals stall, what to do.

2. **Built for the people who fix things, not the people who report on them.** Journey map + playbook = the CS leader can act on this today, not schedule a cross-functional workshop.

3. **Proactive, not reactive.** We map moments that haven't failed yet. That's the distinction between insight and intelligence.

4. **No configuration required.** The AI does the work. You answer questions; we generate the journey, the playbook, the confrontation report.

## Objection Handling

**"We already have [Zendesk / Gainsight / HubSpot]."**  
→ Those tools track tickets and health scores. CX Mate maps the full journey — including moments your support tool never sees. We're the layer above.

**"We don't have budget for another CX tool."**  
→ At $28K/year, Qualtrics isn't your benchmark. We're closer to your post-it budget. And we replace the consultant you'd otherwise hire to do this work.

**"How do we know the journey output is accurate?"**  
→ It's calibrated to your inputs: your stage, your vertical, your customers, your tools. It's not a template — it's your company's CX fingerprint. You'll recognize it in 30 seconds.

**"Our team is lean — we can't implement another platform."**  
→ There's nothing to implement. You answer ~15 questions. We generate the journey map, confrontation report, and playbook. You can be in a conversation with your team about the findings today.
