# CX Mate — Package Value Overview

---

## Free — Try It. No Strings.

**Price:** $0

**What you get:**
- Full customer journey map (sales, customer, or full lifecycle)
- CX Intelligence Report with impact projections
- Prioritized playbook with actionable recommendations
- Evidence Wall connecting insights to your specific pain points
- AI tool recommendations per action
- PDF export
- NotebookLM export (create decks, summaries, infographics)

**The value:**
In minutes — not months — you get what a CX consultant charges $5K-$15K to deliver. A complete CX architecture mapped to your business, with dollar-value impact projections and a step-by-step playbook to act on.

**The limitation:**
One-time snapshot. You can't save, return, or track progress. You see where you stand today — but you can't see if you're getting better.

**Upgrade trigger:** *"I want to track if I'm improving."*

---

## Starter — Your CX Foundation

**Price:** $79/month or $149 one-time

**Everything in Free, plus:**
- Save & return to your journey anytime
- Revenue Protected counter — starts at $0, grows as you complete playbook items
- Monthly CX Score (0-100) — one number for your board, benchmarked against your vertical
- Evidence Wall with ongoing tracking
- Slack nudges — playbook reminders so nothing falls through the cracks

**The value:**
CX Mate becomes your operating system for customer experience. You're not just reading a report — you're executing a playbook and watching the revenue impact compound month over month. The CX Score gives you one number to report to investors or your board without needing CX jargon.

**Who it's for:**
Founders building their first CX process. Heads of CS who need a structured approach. Anyone who wants to move from gut feel to a system.

**Upgrade trigger:** *"I want real data, not estimates."*

---

## Pro — Intelligence Layer

**Price:** $199/month

**Everything in Starter, plus:**
- Monthly Pulse — before/after comparison showing what improved and what needs focus
- HubSpot & Intercom integration — replace benchmark estimates with your real data
- Competitive CX monitoring — how your journey compares to competitors
- AI stack recommendations — which tools to deploy at each stage
- CX simulations — "what if" scenario modeling

**The value:**
This is where CX Mate stops estimating and starts proving. Your HubSpot data validates whether the playbook is actually moving the needle. Competitive monitoring shows where you're winning and where you're exposed. The monthly Pulse creates a compounding record — the longer you use it, the more valuable the trend data becomes.

**Who it's for:**
Growing companies with real customer data. Revenue leaders who need to quantify CX impact. Teams that already use HubSpot or Intercom and want their CX strategy connected to their actual numbers.

**Upgrade trigger:** *"I need to present this to my board."*

---

## Premium — Board-Ready CX

**Price:** $1,200/year

**Everything in Pro, plus:**
- Board Deck generator — auto-generated QBR slides from your CX data
- Multi-seat — your whole team on one account
- QBR reports — quarterly business reviews with CX performance trends
- CRM write-back — push CX insights back into your CRM
- CX Mate MCP server — any AI tool your team uses can query your CX data

**The value:**
CX becomes an org-wide capability, not one person's side project. The Board Deck alone saves 8-12 hours per quarter of manual report building. Multi-seat means your CS team, your product team, and your leadership are all working from the same CX architecture. The MCP server turns CX Mate into the CX brain for every AI tool in your stack.

**Who it's for:**
Scaling companies where CX decisions affect multiple teams. Leaders who report CX performance to a board. Organizations ready to make customer experience a core business function.
