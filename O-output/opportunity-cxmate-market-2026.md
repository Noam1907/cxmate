## 1. What Problem Does CX Mate Solve? (Value)

**The problem in their words:**
> "We know CX matters but we don't have the expertise, tools, or time to do anything about it. Enterprise platforms cost $50K+/year and need a full team. So we wing it — gut feel, spreadsheets, and founder memory — until customers quietly leave."

**Who has this problem:**
- B2B SaaS companies that haven't hired a CX expert yet (stage-based, not size-based)
- Founders, COOs, Heads of CS, CROs who own the customer relationship but lack CX methodology
- Companies at Pre-launch through Growing stages (1–200+ employees, zero CX headcount)

**How painful is it:**
- Poor CX puts **$3.8 trillion in global sales at risk** (Qualtrics/XM Institute)
- **70% of customers** abandon a brand after just 2 bad experiences
- Companies with strong CX strategy see **1.5x higher revenue growth** and **1.8x higher profitability**
- The alternative is a CX consultant at **$150–300/hour** or a CX hire at **$120K–180K/year** — neither accessible to SMBs
- The CX consulting market alone is **$14.4B** — much of it solving this exact problem manually
