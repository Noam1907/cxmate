| 15 | Losing competitive edge | "I believe their future is uncertain as they are clearly losing their competitive advantage" |

### Qualtrics Pricing Reality (2025)
- Median enterprise deal: **~$28K/year** (range $6.5K–$126K)
- Quote-based, no public pricing — frustrating for SMBs
- Self-serve "SMB plan": $420/month billed annually for 1,000 responses
- Even the low end is materially higher than SMB-focused alternatives

### Qualtrics AI — What's There vs SMB Reality
- AI suite: sentiment analysis, predictive intelligence, Stats iQ, "smart recommendations"
- Pricing is interaction-based (higher tiers)
- SMB verdict: "Under-used in small orgs" — requires volume + process maturity most SMBs don't have
- Bottom line: AI is powerful on paper, irrelevant in practice for lean teams
