# CX Mate Beta — Tester Outreach & Follow-Up Sequence
*Created: 2026-03-08 | Owner: Growth / Anat*

---

## Overview

**Goal:** Recruit 15–20 quality beta testers from our ICP (Series A B2B SaaS, early CS teams).
**Exchange:** Full product access (Journey Map + Playbook + CX Review, ~$149 value) for 30 min feedback call.
**Tone:** Peer, not salesperson. Founder to founder. Direct, specific, no fluff.

---

## Touchpoint Sequence

```
Day 0  → First touch (LinkedIn DM or email)
Day 3  → Follow-up if no response
Day 7  → Final nudge (close the loop)
Day 0+ → [After signup] Onboarding FU within 24h
Day 3+ → [After use] Feedback request
Day 7+ → [If no feedback] Reminder + calendar link
```

## Feedback Call Script (30 min)

**Goal:** Understand what resonated, what felt off, and what they'd pay for.

**Minutes 0-5:** Context
- How did you hear about CX Mate?
- What made you try it?

**Minutes 5-15:** Product walkthrough
- Walk me through what you saw. Where did you start?
- What moment made you think "this is useful"?
- What moment made you think "this is wrong" or "this is missing something"?

**Minutes 15-25:** Output quality
- Your journey map — does it feel specific to your company or generic?
- Your playbook — would you actually execute any of these? Which ones?
- Your CX Review — would you share this with your board/investors?

**Minutes 25-30:** Business / closing
- If this cost $149 for one analysis, would you buy it? $99/month ongoing?
- Who else at your company should see this?
- Can I quote anything you said as a testimonial?
