# CX Intel Digest — 2026-02-22

## TL;DR
"Great work + broken operations = silent churn" — empirically validated for SMBs, with the numbers to prove it.

## Signal Bullets

1. **[Research — Pain Validation]** 89% of consumers left a competitor after a poor experience, even when the core product was excellent. — *This IS our core thesis. The sales pitch writes itself: "You're doing great work but losing clients anyway." Direct ICP validation.*

2. **[Research — Revenue Leakage]** SMBs lose 1-9% of total revenue to operational fragmentation. Mid-market: $500K–$2M annually in preventable leakage. — *This is the "dollar number" we need for our hero impact card. CX Mate's ROI story = seal the leakage.*

3. **[Research — SaaS-Specific]** A single missed B2B SaaS renewal = $200K+ annual loss per contract. Involuntary churn from billing errors is a top failure mode. — *We should be explicitly calling this out in the CX Report for SaaS companies. "Renewal risk" moment of truth.*

4. **[Research — Journey Mapping ROI]** Aberdeen Group: Formal journey management → 200% more employee engagement, 3.5x more referral revenue, 18x faster sales cycles, 24.9% marketing ROI growth. — *These are the stats for our "Why journey mapping?" social content and landing page. Credible, quantified, citable.*

5. **[Research — Intuition vs. Data]** Companies using data-driven decisions are 23x more likely to acquire customers, 6x more likely to retain them. But SMBs resist because data feels "cold." — *This is the emotional reframe we need: "We don't replace your intuition — we train it." That's a landing page headline.*

6. **[Research — Moments of Truth]** 65% of companies use journey mapping for competitive advantage; 57% integrate it into CS strategy. Airline case study: fixing airport kiosk friction (not the flight) improved CSAT 28%. — *Counter-intuitive insight: the pain is often not where you think. This validates our Evidence Wall — we surface the non-obvious moments.*

7. **[Research — Agentic AI]** AI agents screen leads 537x faster than humans at 6.9¢ vs $70/search. Resolve 80% of FAQs automatically. Reduce churn up to 20% via predictive scoring. — *For our AI-first lens: every recommendation should ask "can this be agentic?" These numbers validate the automation layer (Scale tier, $499).*

8. **[Research — Onboarding]** SaaS startup that mapped onboarding journey saw 18% churn drop in one year. Automated "48-hour nudge" for non-logins is a specific best-practice. — *We should add "48-hour login nudge" as a specific moment recommendation template in our playbook generator.*

9. **[Research — Retention Math]** CLV = (ARPU × Margin) / Churn Rate. Even a 2-3 point churn rate reduction dramatically increases valuation. — *This is our impact calculator formula made explicit. Should surface this formula in the Assumptions section.*

10. **[Research — Operational Drag]** 60%+ of invoice errors in mid-market companies come from manual processes. Staff waste 1.5–8 hrs/week on avoidable admin. — *Not directly our product, but validates the "operational tax" framing. Good content angle: "How much is your operations team charging your business per week?"*
