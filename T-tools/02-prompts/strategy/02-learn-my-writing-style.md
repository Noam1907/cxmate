Copy and paste this into your chat:

---

```
Read all the writing samples in B-brain/02-writing-samples/ — check ALL subfolders (01-content/, 02-communication/, 03-research/, 04-strategy/) and read whatever samples you find. I may have placed my samples in any of these folders.

B. My DECISION-MAKING STYLE — how I think about choices, how I weigh risk, whether I lead with data or intuition, how I handle uncertainty, how much detail I want.

Then:

1. Update C-core/voice-dna.md - add specific patterns you noticed about how I communicate and think (tone, structure, decision-making signals you picked up)

2. Update A-agents/strategist-agent.md - add a new section called "Owner's Decision Style" with concrete patterns the strategist should follow when presenting analysis to me

3. Update A-agents/chief-of-staff-agent.md - add a new section called "Owner's Brief Preferences" with how I like information presented (level of detail, risk tolerance, data vs intuition balance)

4. Update M-memory/learning-log.md - add an entry documenting what you learned about my style, with specific examples from my samples

Show me a summary of what you learned and what you updated.
```
