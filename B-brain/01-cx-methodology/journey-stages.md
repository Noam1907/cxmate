# B2B SaaS Customer Journey Stages

## Standard Journey Template

### 1. Awareness
- **Description:** First contact with the brand
- **Touchpoints:** Website visit, content engagement, social media, referral
- **Emotional state:** Curious, skeptical
- **Typical duration:** 1-30 days
- **Key signals:** Website visits, content downloads, social follows

### 2. Evaluation
- **Description:** Active consideration and comparison
- **Touchpoints:** Demo, trial, sales conversations, proposal review
- **Emotional state:** Hopeful but cautious
- **Typical duration:** 7-45 days
- **Key signals:** Demo requests, trial signups, pricing page views

### 3. Purchase
- **Description:** Decision and commitment
- **Touchpoints:** Contract, procurement, legal review, payment
- **Emotional state:** Anxious, committed
- **Typical duration:** 1-30 days (varies by deal size)
- **Key signals:** Contract sent, legal questions, payment processing

### 4. Onboarding
- **Description:** Setup and first value delivery
- **Touchpoints:** Account setup, training, first use, activation milestone
- **Emotional state:** Excited but overwhelmed
- **Typical duration:** 14-30 days to first value
- **Key signals:** Login frequency, setup completion, first feature use
- **Critical window:** If no activation by day 30, churn risk increases 3x

### 5. Adoption
- **Description:** Regular usage and expanding value
- **Touchpoints:** Feature discovery, use case expansion, team rollout
- **Emotional state:** Confident, exploring
- **Typical duration:** 30-90 days
- **Key signals:** DAU/WAU, feature breadth, team members added

### 6. Retention
- **Description:** Ongoing engagement and renewal
- **Touchpoints:** Health checks, renewal conversation, upsell discussions
- **Emotional state:** Comfortable (danger: complacent)
- **Typical duration:** Ongoing (renewal cycles)
- **Key signals:** Usage trends, support volume, NPS, renewal timing

### 7. Advocacy
- **Description:** Active promotion and community
- **Touchpoints:** Referrals, case studies, reviews, community participation
- **Emotional state:** Proud, invested
- **Typical duration:** Ongoing
- **Key signals:** Referrals made, reviews written, case study participation

## B2B SaaS Benchmarks

- Average onboarding to first value: 14-30 days
- Critical churn window: Day 30-90 (if no activation)
- Key health signals: Login frequency, feature adoption, support volume
- Common blind spots: Post-sale silence, renewal surprise, champion change
