## Our Core Differentiator

**Competitors are reactive CS tools. CX Mate is a proactive journey design platform.**

- They manage existing customers. We help you design the journey before it goes wrong.
- They require data/CRM integration before delivering value. We deliver value from a single conversation.
- They're built for CS teams. We're built for founders and operational leaders with no CX expert.
- They take 4-24 weeks to implement. We take one conversation.

## Competitor Vulnerabilities (Exploitable in Sales + Positioning)

### Gainsight
- **Vulnerability:** 12-24 week real implementation (vs. 8-week claim). $50K+ annual cost with hidden admin dependency.
- **Who it hurts:** Any company under 200 people — it's overkill.
- **Our angle:** "We deliver what Gainsight promises but only enterprises can afford."

### ChurnZero
- **Vulnerability:** Reporting is the #1 G2 complaint (87+ independent mentions). Board-ready data requires BI tool exports.
- **Secondary:** No mobile app, English-only, requires clean CRM data (not disclosed pre-sale).
- **Our angle:** "ChurnZero tells you what happened. CX Mate tells you why, with the board deck built in."

### Totango
- **Vulnerability:** Post-merger fragmentation (Totango + Catalyst + Unison AI = three separate subscriptions). Support quality has declined since acquisition.
- **Secondary:** Weak Salesforce integration.
- **Our angle:** "Totango is still figuring out who they are after the merger. We know exactly what we do."

### Planhat
- **Vulnerability:** Integration reliability — "The entire tool is super buggy, most integrations do not work as intended." No revenue data integration (a dealbreaker for expansion-focused teams).
- **Secondary:** No free trial. Blank canvas = overwhelming without guidance.
- **Our angle:** "Planhat has great data structure but can't connect to your revenue data. We're built around revenue impact from day one."

## What We Do NOT Compete On

- **Enterprise feature depth** — Gainsight wins on sheer configurability for 1,000+ orgs
- **Existing customer health scoring** — ChurnZero and Planhat have robust health dashboards for CS teams
- **CSM workflow management** — Totango's SuccessBLOCs are a genuine competitive feature

**This is fine.** We win on: journey design (not CS management), time-to-value (minutes not months), no expert required, ROI transparency.
